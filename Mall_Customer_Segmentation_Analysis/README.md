# 🛍️ Mall Customer Segmentation Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing mall customer data to understand customer behavior and provide actionable business insights. 
The goal is to identify patterns in spending habits and suggest effective marketing strategies.

---

## 📊 Dataset Description

The dataset contains the following features:

* **CustomerID** – Unique identifier
* **Gender** – Male/Female
* **Age** – Customer age
* **Annual Income (k$)** – Yearly income
* **Spending Score (1–100)** – Spending behavior score

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Understand customer distribution
* Analyze relationships between features
* Identify customer segments
* Suggest business strategies

---

## 🔍 Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

### ✔️ Statistical Analysis

* Mean, Median, Standard Deviation
* Confidence Interval estimation

### ✔️ Visualization

* Histograms → Distribution of features
* Boxplots → Outliers & skewness
* Correlation Heatmap → Feature relationships

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* **Gender** does not significantly affect spending behavior
* **Younger customers** tend to spend more
* **Spending Score varies independently of income**
* Presence of **high-income outliers**
* Customer behavior is **diverse**, making segmentation useful

---

## 🧪 Hypothesis Testing

* **H₀ (Null Hypothesis):** No difference in spending based on gender
* **Result:** p-value > 0.05
* **Conclusion:** No statistically significant difference between male and female spending behavior

---

## 📊 Confidence Interval

* Estimated average spending score lies between **46 and 53**
* Indicates a reliable range for population mean

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* Target **high income + high spending** customers with premium products
* Use **discounts and offers** for low income but high spending customers
* Engage **high income but low spending** customers using personalized promotions
* Focus on **younger audience** with trendy and digital marketing strategies

Instead, a combination of behavioral and demographic factors should be used to design targeted marketing strategies.
